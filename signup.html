Brantford Master Gardeners include Brantford, St. George, Paris, Glen Morris, Waterford, Hamilton, Burford, and Scotland.


Here’s how to become a Master Gardener:

  • Participate in the recruitment process including an entrance exam and personal interview
  • Begin your apprenticeship following acceptance. This is 2 or 3 years as a master gardener in training
  • Complete either 3 online courses from the University of Guelph or 4 courses from Dalhousie University’s Program. You may have the option of writing a certification exam if you have prior experience.
  • Fulfill mandatory annual volunteering

We are always looking for new Master Gardeners, people who want to learn more about gardening and who are interested in volunteering.
